The Foreign Chapters ABOUT
Venice Mallorca Porto Lisbon Brussels Copenhagen Valencia Madrid Prague

After the whirlwind of Venice, I was thrilled to be heading to Mallorca. I would not only be reuniting with my old NYU roommate, but we were also destined for a tropical getaway where we could soak up the sun. I dragged myself out of bed for a 6:00 AM flight, but even through my sleepy haze on the plane, I could see we were in for a treat. The views were stunning.

My adrenaline was racing and I wanted to be off the airplane as fast as possible to begin the adventures.
