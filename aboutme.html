The Heart of the Story

We often think travel is about seeing famous landmarks. For me, its true magic was the chance to feel things anew—just like a child encountering a world that is still unfamiliar and full of magic.


Travel strips away our "adult" routines and forces us to navigate our environment with a fresh sense of vulnerability and curiosity. This story is about that internal journey.


How to Explore

This isn't a story that unfolds in a straight line. Instead, you’ll explore it through an interactive map of feelings and moments.

Follow your own curiosity by clicking on different locations to unlock the story piece by piece and see my personal growth.

The goal is to make you feel the exhilarating highs and the occasional lonely lows that come with being far from home.

Why It's Different

In a world of highly edited and polished content, My Foreign Chapters offers something different. This is a deliberately raw and interactive journey. It’s an invitation to slow down, explore at your own pace, and perhaps be inspired to rediscover the magic in your own everyday world.
