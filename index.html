This is More Than a Travel Log

It's a travel journal turned immersive digital story about rediscovering wonder through travel.

The Concept

My Foreign Chapters is an immersive digital story and interactive travel journal. It's a raw and intimate look at my study abroad experience and future travels, designed to make you feel like you're walking alongside me.


But it's more than just a record of places I've been. At its heart, this is a story about rediscovering the whimsy and wonder we often leave behind in childhood.

I know the path where whimsy grows. Follow me.
