The Foreign Chapters About
Venice Mallorca Porto Lisbon Brussels Copenhagen Valencia Madrid Prague

I allowed myself to arrive in Lisbon with expectations. I was determined to catch a picturesque sunset from one of the many viewpoints Lisbon had to offer. Having arrived via bus around

2:00 PM the wait for sunset would not be long, I took advantage of the time to wander around. I stumbled upon a flea market where I pondered in awe of all the trinkets for sale.

With anticipation brewing I scouted out a few viewpoints to go visit before sunset time, stoping to try as many pasteis de nata as I could hold down along the way.


As a student on a budget traveling I quickly realized the best way into these foreign places would be through the sweet treats they had to offer. Oddly enough this made me feel like a rebelious child that had no one to tell them to stop consuming sugar.

At last it was time for the main star of the show, having stopped to grab a quick bite for dinner I almost missed it. The sunset was perfect, the ambiance was unbelievable there were musicians performing live as a bunch of us tourists collectively admired the beauty. Lisbon lived up to its hype about sunset watching. This was the best sunset watching experience I have ever had, it was straight out of a movie.
